Services

As an independent GIS software consultant and developer, I help businesses harness the power of geospatial data through data ingestion, analysis, visualization, and system integration. I design and build solutions that make location-based data actionable, whether for internal use, customer-facing applications, or third-party integrations. The key areas of focus are outlined below.

  • Geospatial Data Ingestion

    Geospatial data from various sources—such as OpenStreetMap, WorldPop, and custom datasets—can be seamlessly integrated into existing business systems or dedicated data stores to enhance analysis and visualization. The process ensures that data is accurate, scalable, and efficiently handled, whether it needs to be synchronized with third-party services or processed through tailored data workflows.

  • Geographical Data Processing & Analysis

    Spatial data can be analyzed to uncover trends and support informed decision-making across various industries. Techniques such as data grouping, enrichment, and trend analysis help turn raw geographical data into meaningful business insights that improve efficiency and strategic planning.

  • Custom Web Applications and Widgets

    The development of interactive geospatial applications enables users to engage with spatial data through custom-built maps, real-time data tables, and navigation tools. Solutions range from small embedded widgets to full-fledged web applications, ensuring adaptability to various use cases.

  • Printable Geospatial Data Presentation

    High-quality printable exports are generated in formats such as PDF and Excel to support reporting, compliance, and offline analysis. Customizable map designs ensure that spatial data can be effectively communicated in print-ready formats, tailored to meet specific business needs.

  • Third-Party BI & System Integration

    The integration of geospatial data with Business Intelligence (BI) platforms like Looker, as well as with third-party APIs, enhances analytics and decision-making capabilities. The embedding of location-based insights into BI ecosystems ensures greater value extraction from spatial data.
